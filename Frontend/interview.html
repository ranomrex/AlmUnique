Navigating the Future of Generative Systems

A deep dive into how large language models are reshaping the backend landscape.

Q: Where do developers get it wrong when integrating AI into backends?

The mistake isn't in the code; it's in the architecture. You can't treat an LLM like a deterministic database. You have to design for failure and latency from the ground up.

"The most important skill of the next decade isn't coding—it's curiosity."

Q: Why is curiosity becoming more valuable than technical skill?

Because technical "how-to" is being democratized. Knowing *what* to ask and *why* we build it is where the real value now resides.
